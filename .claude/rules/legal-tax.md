### Required Contract Clauses (MA)
- Governing law: Commonwealth of Massachusetts
- Dispute resolution: mediation before litigation
- IP ownership: work-for-hire language for deliverables
- Limitation of liability: cap at fees paid in prior 3 months
- Payment terms: Net 30 default; late fee 1.5%/month
- Termination: 30-day written notice by either party

## Tax Considerations (Massachusetts)

- **Sales tax:** MA does not tax most consulting/professional services — note exceptions for software implementation
- **Estimated taxes:** Quarterly federal (Form 1040-ES) and MA (Form 1-ES) if operating as sole prop / LLC
- **1099 reporting:** Issue 1099-NEC to contractors paid ≥ $600/year
- **MA income tax rate:** 5% flat (as of 2024; confirm current rate)
- **Business entity:** Track entity type (LLC, S-Corp, etc.) — affects self-employment tax treatment
- **Home office deduction:** Document square footage and exclusive use
