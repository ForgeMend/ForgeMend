Our Story

About ForgeMend

Built around one insight: most ERP implementations fail because of data, not software. Serving clients across the US and globally.

We've Seen Every Flavor of Messy Data — And We Know How to Fix It

ForgeMend was founded on a premise that every Sage X3 consultant eventually learns the hard way: a perfect ERP configuration on top of bad data is still a failed implementation.

After seeing go-live after go-live struggle with duplicate customer records, unmapped UOM codes, incorrectly structured item masters, and import files that didn't match the Sage X3 spec — we built ForgeMend to solve that specific problem.

We specialize in the unglamorous, mission-critical work that happens before the system goes live: cleaning the data, building the pipelines, mapping the fields, and making sure every import file is exactly right before it touches your production environment.

ForgeMend provides Sage X3 implementation, data cleanup, UOM conversions, customer and supplier imports, and ERP consulting — specializing in food & beverage manufacturing as well as general manufacturing and distribution businesses. Serving clients remotely across the US and globally.
