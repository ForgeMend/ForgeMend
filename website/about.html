Most Sage X3 projects hit the same wall: the data from the old system doesn't match what Sage X3 expects. Fields are in the wrong format. UOM codes don't align. Duplicate records are everywhere. Import files fail validation.

ForgeMend was built to solve exactly that problem. We take your old ERP exports — whatever format they're in — and your Sage X3 import templates, and we deliver validated CSV files that are mapped to your exact environment and ready to import.

We deliver files, not consulting engagements. That focus means every project is the same disciplined process: map the fields, fix the data, validate the output, and hand it over with a clear import guide.

Hands-on Sage X3 data operations experience in food & beverage and manufacturing environments. Strong background in product, customer, and supplier master data — including complex UOM conversions, field mapping, deduplication, and cleanup across legacy ERP systems including QuickBooks, SAP Business One, and Dynamics NAV.
