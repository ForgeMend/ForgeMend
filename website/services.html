Data Cleanup & Migration

Messy master data is the #1 cause of Sage X3 go-live failures. We find it, fix it, and give you a clean, validated dataset before a single record touches your ERP.

✓

Customer & supplier normalization: Standardize names, addresses, codes, and contact data across all records.

✓

Deduplication with merge logic: Identify and resolve duplicate records using configurable matching keys — no silent drops.

✓

Item master cleanup: UOM alignment, description standardization, product hierarchy correction, and inactive item handling.

✓

Row-level error reporting: Every skipped or modified record is documented in the audit log with the reason.

03
Core Service

UOM Conversions

Unit-of-measure errors compound silently until go-live, when they surface as pricing mismatches, costing errors, and picking problems. We fix them at the source.

✓

Full catalog UOM mapping: Every item mapped from source UOM to Sage X3 standard codes (EA, CS, LB, KG, GL, LT, PL, and more).

✓

Conversion factor validation: Factors verified against industry standards; rounded to 6 decimal places maximum.

✓

Sage X3 reference alignment: All UOM codes verified against your Sage X3 UOM reference table before output.

✓

Multi-level conversions: Sales UOM, purchasing UOM, and stocking UOM hierarchies handled correctly.

Core Service

Customer & Supplier Imports

We build purpose-built pipelines that transform your raw data into import-ready CSV files that match the Sage X3 BPCUSTOMER and BPSUPPLIER specifications exactly.

✓

Field-level mapping: Every source field mapped to the exact Sage X3 column, with length limits and code validation enforced.

✓

Payment terms & tax codes: PTE and tax ID fields validated against your Sage X3 reference data.

✓

Address standardization: USPS-style address normalization, state codes, and ZIP+4 handling.

✓

Repeatable pipelines: Scripts delivered with your project — rerun any time your source data changes.
