Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What do I need to submit a project?

Two things: your Sage X3 import template (exported from Setup → Usage → Imports/Exports, or provided by your Sage partner) and a rough sense of your record counts. You share the actual data only after we've agreed on price and scope.

Where do you serve clients?

Everywhere. We work remotely with clients across the United States and internationally. Sage X3 data engineering doesn't require a physical presence — it requires deep expertise, clear communication, and deliverables that are right the first time.

How do you price engagements?

Most data cleanup and import projects are fixed-fee based on scope. We review your template and send a written quote within 1 business day — no hourly surprises.

We're mid-project and our import files are failing. Can you still help?

Yes. This is a common situation — the further along you are, the more urgent it is. If your import files are failing validation or your data quality review is raising red flags, share your template and we'll tell you what it takes to fix it.

Do you work with Sage X3 partners/VARs?

Yes. We work alongside existing Sage X3 VARs and their clients as a data specialist — we're not trying to replace your VAR, we're delivering the clean import files they need.

What formats do you work with?

We accept data in CSV, Excel (.xlsx), and flat text files. We work with any source system that can export in those formats — legacy ERP, accounting systems, spreadsheets, and more.
