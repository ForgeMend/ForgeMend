Common Questions

Before You Reach Out

What should I bring to the discovery call?

Nothing formal required. It helps to have a rough sense of your record counts (customers, suppliers, items), your Sage X3 version, and your go-live target date — but we can figure the rest out together.

Where do you serve clients?

Everywhere. We work remotely with clients across the United States and internationally. Sage X3 data engineering doesn't require a physical presence — it requires deep expertise, clear communication, and deliverables that are right the first time.

How do you price engagements?

Most data cleanup and import projects are fixed-fee based on scope. We provide a written quote after the discovery call — no hourly surprises.

We're already mid-implementation. Can you still help?

Absolutely — mid-implementation rescues are common. If your import files are failing or your data quality review is raising red flags, the sooner we look at it, the better.

Do you work with Sage X3 partners/VARs?

Yes. We work alongside existing Sage X3 VARs as a data engineering specialist — we're not trying to replace your implementation partner, we're helping them deliver better data.

What formats do you work with?

We accept data in CSV, Excel (.xlsx), and flat text files. We work with any source system that can export in those formats — legacy ERP, accounting systems, spreadsheets, and more.
